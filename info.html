Similarity index between projects


The similarity index shows the ratio of the shared vs the total value tags of the compared projects


The index shown in the "project - similarity" column of the table relies on the tags related to each project and based on the Jaccard coeficcient

The Jaccard coefficient measures similarity between finite sample sets, and is defined as the size of the intersection divided by the size of the union of the sample sets

Jaccard Index (Wikipedia)

The sample sets are the sets of tags linked to the projects

The second column "tags" lists all the tags that are in common (intersection of the sets) between the two projects - the selected one and the one in the left column of the table



For example, the project ADVANCE has 3 tags:

    Vaccines
    Infectious diseases
    Methods for benefit/risk assessment

the project ZAPI has 2 tags:

    Vaccines
    Infectious diseases

in total there are 3 different tags and two of them are shared between both projects

this gives a jaccard coeficcient of 2/3, rounded 67%

the similarity index between both projects is 67%
